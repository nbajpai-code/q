# Quantum Computing Courses & Resources

A curated list of learning resources for Quantum Computing, ranging from beginner introductions to advanced algorithms and hardware.

## üèÜ IBM Quantum Learning
*The premier destination for learning Qiskit and IBM's quantum stack.*

### Course Series: Quantum Information & Computation (with John Watrous)
1.  **[Basics of Quantum Information](https://quantum.cloud.ibm.com/learning/courses/basics-of-quantum-information)**
    *   *Level*: Beginner
    *   *Focus*: States, measurements, quantum circuits, and entanglement. (15 hours)
2.  **[Fundamentals of Quantum Algorithms](https://quantum.cloud.ibm.com/learning/courses/fundamentals-of-quantum-algorithms)**
    *   *Level*: Intermediate
    *   *Focus*: Integer factoring, search algorithms, and speedups over classical. (15 hours)
3.  **[General Formulation of Quantum Information](https://quantum.cloud.ibm.com/learning/courses/general-formulation-of-quantum-information)**
    *   *Level*: Advanced
    *   *Focus*: Density matrices, channels, and general measurements. (15 hours)
4.  **[Foundations of Quantum Error Correction](https://quantum.cloud.ibm.com/learning/courses/foundations-of-quantum-error-correction)**
    *   *Level*: Advanced
    *   *Focus*: Protecting against noise, error correcting codes, and fault tolerance. (15 hours)

### Key Techniques & Applications
-   **[Quantum Machine Learning](https://quantum.cloud.ibm.com/learning/courses/quantum-machine-learning)**: Leverage quantum computing in ML methods. (10 hours)
-   **[Quantum Chemistry with VQE](https://quantum.cloud.ibm.com/learning/courses/quantum-chem-with-vqe)**: Introduction to Variational Quantum Eigensolver. (5 hours)
-   **[Utility-scale Quantum Computing](https://quantum.cloud.ibm.com/learning/courses/utility-scale-quantum-computing)**: 14-lesson course on working with 100+ qubits. (15 hours)
-   **[Practical Introduction to Quantum Safe Cryptography](https://quantum.cloud.ibm.com/learning/courses/quantum-safe-cryptography)**: Understanding post-quantum cryptography risks and solutions. (10 hours)
